About Us

  • CTI is the umbrella of CTH and CGA.
  • CTI means Citadelle Tech Institute.
  • CTH means Citadelle Tech Hub.
  • CGA means Citadelle Graphic Agency
  • CTH is in charge of the FREE annual
    skill acquisition programs.
  • These are all under the umbrella, CTI

CTH hold FREE annual tech bootcamps (skill acquisition programs) for specially undergraduates and interested graduates. In this bootcamp, basic training on tech courses are being held for a number of days, projects are executed and the bests are certified freely.

After the training, a grand finale summit is held, where projects are exhibited, graduands are presented, certificates are given, awards are granted, investments are made, all to culminate the program.
